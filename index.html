Batman Arkham Knight - The night rises

September 20, 2019

Batman Arkham Knight feels like a finale to Rocksteady’s Batman series. It combines all the best aspects of its predecessors, to create a pretty accurate representation of the Batman. The world is larger than ever before and is home to some of Batman’s closest allies and rogues. With the addition of an interesting but fully playable Batmobile, Arkham Knight is one of the best Batman games to date.
